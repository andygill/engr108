Help for Escaping from Crater Lake

Instructions

Welcome to Escape from Crater Lake!

  • Your objective is to row off the lake, without Bigfoot catching you.
  • You can control your direction.
  • If you land on a red segment, you lose.
  • You have one minute.
  • If you succeed, then Bigfoot gets faster.

Good luck!

Programming the Boat

Assign pos with the value of the direction you want to go, in radians.

EastNorthWestSouth
PIPI/20-PI/2

There are a number of built in variables and functions that you can used.

Variables

At the start of your program, the old value of pos is already set.

x and y are the location of your boat on the screen, with 0,0 being the center of the lake.

PI is predefined as the value of π.

keyXisPressed and keyZisPressed are set if 'X' or 'Z' are pressed, respectively.

monster is the location of the monster, in radians.

Functions

The trigonometry sin( ... ), cos( ... ), atan2( ... , ... ) are available for you to use.

Sample programs

Follow the mouse

controlX = mouseX - x;
controlY = mouseY - y;
dir = atan2(controlY,controlX);

Beeline the other way

pos = PI;
